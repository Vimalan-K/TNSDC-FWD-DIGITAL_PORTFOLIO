Since its inception in 1853 with the first passenger train from Mumbai to Thane, Indian Railways has evolved into one of the world's largest railway networks under single management.

Historic Milestones

1853

First Train Journey

The first passenger train ran between Mumbai (Bori Bunder) and Thane, marking the beginning of railways in India.

1988

Shatabdi Express Launch

Introduction of high-speed day trains connecting major cities with enhanced comfort and reduced travel time.

2019

Vande Bharat Era

Launch of India's first semi-high-speed train, showcasing indigenous manufacturing and modern technology.

2025

100% Electrification Target

Achieving complete electrification of the railway network, making it one of the world's largest green transport systems.
